Proposal

Using WHOIS database of newly registered domains to compile and generate a list of potential leads for web products and services

What is a WHOIS Database Download?

Whois downloads provide complete historic domain data, such as registrant name, e-mail & registration address, organization, registrar creation, expiration, and updated dates, among other data points.
